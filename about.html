Real Madrid has won La Liga 34 times and the Copa del Rey (King's Cup) 19 times, the most out of any team in the league. It has also won the UEFA Champions League a record 14 times and the UEFA Cup twice. Real Madrid was recognised as the FIFA Club of the 20th Century on 11 December 2000, and received the FIFA Centennial Order of Merit on 20 May 2002.
In June 2017, the team succeeded in becoming the first club to win the Champions League twice in a row.
In 2018, Madrid made history by becoming the first club in recent history to win the Champions League three times in a row.
